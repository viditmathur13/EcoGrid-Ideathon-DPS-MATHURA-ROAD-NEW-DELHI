Research



  • Our team of experts, in collaboration with marine specialists, is developing UNDERWATER DATA CENTERS . This project explores the feasibility of submerging data centers and evaluates their potential for energy efficiency and sustainability. Initial results show improved performance and reliability, as underwater deployment offers several key advantages:

    1. → Enhanced cooling - The naturally cold ocean environment efficiently cools the servers, reducing the need for traditional energy-intensive cooling systems.

    2. → Greater reliability - Being submerged protects the infrastructure from dust, temperature fluctuations, and human interference.

    3. → Faster deployment - Modular designs can be quickly assembled and positioned near coastal urban centers, minimizing latency.
